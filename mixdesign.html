Event Description

To build a needful infrastructure it requires an appropriate concrete mix design. CMD (Concrete Mix-Design) involves mixing of materials together in a proper ratio to get required strength and durability as per our requirements for the concrete. Because every material has their own/different properties. To get perfect CMD requires a perfect knowledge about all the material which are going to use in concrete.

CMD chosen depending on the needs of the project as well depends on Building codes for example in India we require IS codes that is Indian standard codes like for mix design IS 456 (plain and reinforced concrete) is use this is just a one example for every material some standard criteria are fixed which are mentioned in IS codes. Strength, Workability and Durability are the main criteria of any mix design, Concrete strength is measured in N/mm2. To fulfill requirement in structures only cement, water, Fine Aggregate, Coarse Aggregate is not important we need to add some extra admixtures in concrete to get our structure. (For examples in our event we require a lightweight concrete to get it we require some admixtures as well some different aggregates). We also need to ensure that to fulfill our structural needs we should not hamper the concrete strength and its other properties.
