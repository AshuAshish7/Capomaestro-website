Symbiosis Institute of Technology

11th Year of
Capomaestro

CAPOMAESTRO on completing a decade is all set to climb unusual heights with various new events and competitions. Capomestro provides a unique platform for aspiring civil engineers to explore and acquire the immensity of the stream. It encourages and promotes students to innovate, establish and perceive interesting events and competitions.

Symbiosis Institute of Technology, a constituent of Symbiosis International University was established in the year, 2008. The Institute endeavors to provide quality technical education in line with the requirements of today's competitive industry and fast-paced technological developments.
